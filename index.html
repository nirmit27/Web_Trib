Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

" Missile Man of India, The People's President "


Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. He had a keen interest in literature and wrote poems. He never married. He always faced media himself for his failures while let others address for the success. He remained active till the very end. He died while delivering a lecture at the IIM, Shillong.

"If you want to shine like a sun, first burn like a sun."
- Dr. A.P.J. Kalam
